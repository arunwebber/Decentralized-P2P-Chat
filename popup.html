Stranger P2P Chat

Anonymous • P2P • No central logs

Idle
Manual signaling (serverless)

How to connect:

  1. Person A: Click "Start" to generate an offer
  2. Person A: Copy and share the offer with Person B
  3. Person B: Paste the offer and click "Generate Answer"
  4. Person B: Copy and share the answer back to Person A
  5. Person A: Paste the answer and click "Apply Answer"

Tracker mode requires adding `lib/simplepeer.min.js` and `lib/bittorrent-tracker.min.js` to enable auto-matching.

WebRTC P2P chat with manual or tracker signaling. No central message storage. Use 'Next' to leave & start a new session.

Features: Text chat, audio/video calls, screen sharing, and file transfer.

To enable tracker auto-match, drop the browser builds of simple-peer and bittorrent-tracker into lib/ (see README).
